Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to inform people in the teens to adult age the fun and excitement they can experience at this new water rafting place. We want to inform them what they will be expecting, what we will provide them with and how much it will cost.

Audience

The audience that we are taking in mind is the people in the teens to adult age. as well as the boy scout age. we want to wow them and have them come and experience this great place. Our content will have pictures of the place and the rafting so people can expect what they will experience at this new attraction. we will also have nice vibrant colors to both catch the attention of people and have them informed of what this site is and it is eazy to use. if there needs are not being met they will have the oppertunity to share feedback and if severe we can give them a free pass to have them come back for free or refund.
