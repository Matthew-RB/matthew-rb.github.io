Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to introduce teens and adults who are either new or love thriller the wonders of riding a roller coaster. this webiste will prepare audience what they will be expecting on a roller coaster, how much it cost, and the prerequisites of the rides and how tall you need to be in order to ride it. this website will be friendly for both people who are looking for an exciteing ride, or people who are looking for a nice starting ride.

Audience

The audience that we are looking for is people from teens to adult age. we also want to target people who are wanting to try out roller coasters and people who want to find the best roller coasters. our content will have pictures of different roller coasters and also have pictures of smileing people. we want to wow people and want to make them excited to ride roller coasters. we want to also have nice and vibrant colors that will catch the audiences attention.

Home page

People will come to the roller coaster website because they want to find fun and exciting roller coasters across the world. People want to experience thriller like they have never seen before, and they could come to this site to find out what is the ride is and the requirements to get on the roller coaster. They could also see if the ride requires any payment or where the roller coaster is.

The information to complete any action on our site would be the name of the roller coaster, where the roller coaster is, And the type of thrill ride. they can choose either one of these options and our website will list roller coasters in that area. if they wish to sign up, they will need their email and a password to sign up and they can write down their list of roller coasters they ride on.

People will want to come to this website instead of other websites because this website will have everything. Instead of going to multiple websites trying to find multiple roller coasters, they can come to this website, and we will have everything that they will need. with this website they can search multiple roller coasters for free, and they do not need to have the hassle of going to multiple websites trying to find what roller coaster they have been on.

Features

The type of activities visitors will want to complete is the EXPLORE option. With the explore option, users will have the opportunity to search up their favorite roller coaster and find the information that they want to get. the explore option can also find roller coasters that you have not been on two. users can also SIGN UP and add onto the LIST OF ROLLER COASTER you have been on so our website can help you find roller coasters you have not been on and would like to try to ride the roller coaster.

People can also come to this website to experience a THRILL SCALE, where we will list roller coasters by the amount of thrill that they have. this way people who are wanting to look for the best thrill rights can search them right away, and those who are new to roller coasters can start off their day on a less thrill ride. That way everyone is happy for both new commers and veterans to roller coasters.

Comments and questions

The questions that visitors might expect from the site will be where is the roller coaster? how much would the roller coaster cost? what are the requirements to get on the ride? Is the roller coaster good? Does the roller coaster have any sudden drops or stops? What did other people think about the roller coaster? Is the website friendly? Is the website easy to use? all these questions will be answered in our website.

The audience may want to read about the fun reviews that people say about the roller coaster and about the bad reviews so that they know what they are going to expect when they go to the ride and have a pre-expectation of the ride that it will be good or bad. People can also read about the description of the ride that the original ride has. If people want to search about the theme park roller coasters like Disney Land, they can research that, and the website will also inform them that the ride is in Disney Land or World, and they need to have tickets to the theme park before they can ride the Roller Coaster. It will also have the height limit to ride the ride and any information that the roller coaster may give out such as sudden drops or that you need to not have any loose items in your pockets.
