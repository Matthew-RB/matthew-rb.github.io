Barnstormer at Walt Disney World's Magic Kingdom

For a beginner roller coaster, Barnstormer at World Magic Kingdom would be one of the many options that you can take your child on. of course this would not be your only option but it would be Recommended to have a roller coaster that will not have lots of drops or sudden stops and that it is not to fast.

For a intermediate coaster. family members should try roller coasters that would have some high speeds with not so much drops and stops. This would have the person get used to a little more speed on coasters with little drops to get used to it as well. The intermediate coasters would be about any coaster that is not a top thrill ride.

Manta, Sea World Orlando

Advanced coasters are for people who want to experience true speed and high drops and sudden stops. these are people who want to experience the thrill and want to find more fun. these are coasters that are among the top thrill rides recorded. one of the advanced coasters that is fun for everyone is the Manta coaster in Sea World, Orlando.
